Our Story

From a backyard experiment to a neighborhood hearth

Sol & Ember began as a passion project by Chef Marin Solano — a desire to reconnect cooking with flame, place, and seasonality. We partner with local farms, practice regenerative buying, and celebrate the transformative flavor of fire. Our tasting menu changes weekly. We balance textural contrasts, char, smoke, and bright seasonal produce to create memorable meals for friends and strangers alike.
